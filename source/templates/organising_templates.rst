How to organise templates 
=========================

1) Navigate to **Templates** and click **Create Folder**.

   .. image:: ./images/create_folder.png
      :alt: Create template folder
      :width: 100%

2) Assign a meaningful name to your folder. Optionally, you can also pick a colour and specify a description. Then, click **Create**.

   .. image:: ./images/create_folder2.png
      :alt: Assign template folder name
      :width: 100%

3) Click and drag a template into its respective folder. 

   .. image:: ./images/click_and_drag_template.png
      :alt: Click and drag template into its respective folder
      :width: 100%

4) Your template should now reside in its intended folder.

   .. image:: ./images/template_final.png
      :alt: View template inside its folder
      :width: 100%
