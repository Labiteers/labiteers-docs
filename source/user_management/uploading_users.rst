Upload users 
============

1) Navigate to **Users** and click **Download Template**.

    .. image:: ./images/download_template.png
       :alt: Download template for uploading users
       :width: 100%

2) Populate the template with your students' details.

3) To upload your list of users, go to **Users** and click the **Upload (up arrow)** icon.

    .. important::
        The users must be uploaded in the format specified in the downloaded template.

    .. image:: ./images/upload_csv_file.png
       :alt: Upload users
       :width: 100%
