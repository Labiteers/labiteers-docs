Create an account
=================

1) Navigate to the signup page.

   .. image:: ./images/create_account.png
      :alt: Create VM menu
      :width: 60%

2) Fill in the required fields by supplying the following details:

- Your full name
- Your email address
- Your password
- Your card number

3) Click **Create Account**.

.. note::

   After creating an account, you will automatically start a 7-day free trial without being charged.
   At the end of the trial, you must subscribe to a plan to continue using the platform.
